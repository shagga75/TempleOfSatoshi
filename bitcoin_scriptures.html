GENESIS: THE ORIGINAL BLOCK

In the beginning, the world was plunged into financial darkness, controlled by centralized banks and governments. And then, in the year 2008, an anonymous prophet, Satoshi Nakamoto, delivered the sacred Whitepaper to the world: "Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System."

And in the year 2009, the Genesis Block was mined, the first link in an incorruptible chain, and inscribed in its code was the prophetic message: "The Times 03/Jan/2009 Chancellor on brink of second bailout for banks." And thus, the network was born, illuminating the path for men and women towards financial sovereignty.

The true bitcoiner does not pursue immediate riches, but long-term financial freedom. They do not fear volatility, for they understand that price is fleeting, but the revolution is eternal. They understand that Proof of Work is the only valid sacrifice for the validation of truth.
