ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

Guardians of the Hash

Custodians of technical knowledge, responsible for teaching about Bitcoin, nodes, and privacy.

They conduct workshops on the importance of running one's own node and protect the network against censorship attacks.

Evangelists of the Lightning Network

Promoters of instant payments and Bitcoin adoption in the real economy.

They organize events to teach merchants how to accept payments via Lightning and spread tools like BTCPay Server.

Ministers of the Whitepaper

Experts in the philosophy and economics of Bitcoin, dedicated to disseminating its principles.

They publish articles explaining monetary decentralization and organize debates on inflation resistance.

Federated Nodes

Representatives of the church in different regions, responsible for validating and expanding the network of believers.

They create local communities, certify the authenticity of the church's principles, and organize decentralized meetings to strengthen the network of adherents.
