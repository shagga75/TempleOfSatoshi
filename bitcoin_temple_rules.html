Community Rules

Mutual Respect:
All members must treat each other with respect and courtesy, regardless of their opinions or beliefs.
Focus on Bitcoin and Decentralization:
Discussions should center on Bitcoin, privacy, individual sovereignty, and topics related to financial freedom.
Promotion of Education:
The constructive exchange of knowledge is encouraged, without belittling those who are learning.
Responsible Use of Space:
Taking care of shared facilities and tools is the responsibility of all members (if the space is physical).
Collaboration and Community:
Cooperation among participants is encouraged to strengthen the community.
Exclusive Use of Bitcoin:
All transactions within the temple must be made exclusively in Bitcoin or via the Lightning Network.
Privacy and Security:
Personal data of other members should not be shared without their consent.
Self-Management and Transparency:
Members can propose improvements or initiatives for the temple's growth.

Prohibitions

No "crypto" or NFT discussions allowed:
This space is dedicated exclusively to Bitcoin and its ecosystem.
No discussion of religion or politics:
To avoid division, these topics should be addressed outside the temple.
Violence and harassment are prohibited:
Any form of aggression, discrimination, or hostile behavior will result in immediate expulsion.
Sale of drugs, weapons, or any illegal activity is prohibited:
The Temple of Satoshi does not tolerate illicit activities under any circumstances.
Promotion of fraudulent schemes is not allowed:
Any attempt at deception, pyramid schemes, or scams will be sanctioned.
Spam or unauthorized advertising is not allowed:
The community should focus on genuine learning and cooperation.
Doxxing is prohibited:
Personal data of other members cannot be disclosed without their consent.
No use of fiat money inside the temple:
All transactions must be conducted in Bitcoin.
Toxic discussions or personal attacks are not tolerated:
Healthy and well-reasoned debates are welcome, but toxicity is not.
No cameras or recordings without permission:
Privacy is a fundamental pillar and all attendees must be respected. (In virtual meetings, the privacy option is enabled; you can choose an avatar or another image.)
Failure to comply with these rules may result in warnings, temporary suspension, or permanent expulsion from the temple.
These rules are subject to review and improvement by the community.

Live the principles of decentralization and freedom responsibly!
