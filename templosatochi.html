Rituals and Practices

The Offering Transaction

As an act of faith, members can make donations in Bitcoin to sustain the community and promote the adoption of digital currency. (NFC cards can be provided)

The On-Chain Confirmation

Each new member is welcomed with their first transaction on the network, symbolizing their entry into the decentralized community.

The Genesis Block

We celebrate January 3rd as the sacred day the first Bitcoin block was mined, marking the beginning of the financial revolution.

The Node of Wisdom

Every temple or meeting point must operate a Bitcoin node and share knowledge on how to use and safeguard funds without intermediaries. (One can be donated)

Verification, Not Faith

We encourage self-education and the use of verification tools. We don't believe in promises, only cryptographic proofs.

Code of Conduct

Respect for Decentralization

Every member must act in favor of decentralization and financial autonomy.

Knowledge Sharing

It is the duty of members to educate others about Bitcoin and its benefits.

No Censorship

We defend freedom of speech and the right to privacy on the network.

Responsible Use of Technology

We promote the ethical use of Bitcoin and cryptographic tools.
